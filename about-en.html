About the factory "therma"

In 1993, the factory began its activities with the production and sale of dairy products (butter, ghee), pistachios, walnuts, raisins.

The factory under the leadership of Weissy Topuza has expanded its product range by selling products own production in 2005 of herbal pastes, as well as herbal tea and powder, in 2006 cosmetic creams and oils for skin care of plant origin, in 2007 the product line "Fiyto" (shampoos, lotions, gels of plant origin)..

The factory, which has been operating for 17 years, continues to expand the range products. The head of the factory, who manufactures products that meet international standards, is aimed to expand production in other countries, taking advantage of the success in their homeland.

About the factory "Mustafa Bey"

Founder of the production company "Mustafa Bey" - Mustafa Topuz, since 1999 engaged in various types of commercial activities, then from 2007 he continued his career in a company whose main activity was the production of products from natural products and medicinal herbs, and in his work he rose to the level of the head of the company. With extensive knowledge and rich experience in the sector, having an understanding of the needs of the natural products market and understanding in what direction to develop its activities, Mustafa Topuz in 2018 created his company under brand "Mustafa Bey Bitkisel Ürünler Dünyası".

As soon as a new company with a manager with many years of experience entered the market of natural products, it won success and in a short time began to produce products, both for Turkey and other countries. The company exports its products to countries such as Germany, Belgium, the Netherlands, Ukraine, Iraq, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Russia ...

Topuz intends to continue this success in other countries, and in the near future plans show the quality of your products to the whole world.
